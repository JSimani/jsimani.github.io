Biography

Hi! My name is Josh Simani. I am a 20-year old college student studying Computer Science at Tufts University in Massachusetts. I was born and raised in Needham, Massachusetts, which is only 45 minutes away from campus. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with friends, watching sports, and spending time with children. For the past four years, I have worked at my childhood summer camp in New Hampshire as a camp counselor, working with 7- to 14-year olds across the camp. I also volunteer at the local Elementary School through the Leonard Carmichael Society to provide a fun after-school experience for children living in Medford.
